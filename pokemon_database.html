FireRed & LeafGreen Pokédex

FireRed & LeafGreen stick to the original 151 Pokémon of Red/Blue/Yellow, despite having introduced two new generations of Pokémon in the meantime, including evolutions of Kanto Pokémon. Said Pokémon (such as Crobat, Pichu) are only obtainable after the National Dex is acquired.
